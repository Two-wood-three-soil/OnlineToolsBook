FAQ

Dunkin bucket availability

Does this page show live Dunkin inventory?

No. It is a fast lookup tool for finding the right stores to call. Live limited-item inventory usually depends on each franchise location.

What should I ask the store?

Ask whether the bucket is in stock, whether it is available today, whether a drink or meal purchase is required, and whether there is a limit per customer.

Why can availability change so quickly?

Limited merch can sell out, arrive late, or be handled differently by individual stores. Calling first saves the trip.
